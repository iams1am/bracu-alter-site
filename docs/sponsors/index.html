2026 Sponsors

Our 2026 sponsors are fueling the next chapter of autonomous rescue robotics at BRACU ALTER. Their support enables us to push the boundaries of what our rovers can achieve on the global stage.

Title Sponsors

BRAC University

BRAC University provides institutional support, lab facilities, and funding that forms the backbone of BRACU ALTER's operations. Our home at the BSRM School of Engineering gives us the infrastructure to innovate.

LaSSET

The Laboratory of Space Systems Engineering & Technology (LaSSET) is our research home, providing dedicated lab space, equipment, mentorship, and the academic framework for our rescue robotics research.
