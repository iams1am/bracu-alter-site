**BRACU ALTER** is a student-led research team from the Laboratory of Space System Engineering & Technology (LaSSET) within the BSRM School of Engineering at BRAC University.

We develop advanced, modular rescue rovers and AGVs designed for surveillance, inspection, and high-risk search and rescue operations. Our platforms are engineered for power efficiency and reliable navigation in rough, disaster-prone terrain—supporting faster and safer response when every second matters.

## Specialties
Autonomous Guided Vehicles (AGVs), Search & Rescue Robotics, Disaster Response Technology, AI-Powered Navigation, Real-Time Data Systems, Hazardous Terrain Mobility, Modular Rover Design, Embedded Systems, Sensor Integration, Robotics Research, Humanitarian Engineering, Mechatronics Innovation.
