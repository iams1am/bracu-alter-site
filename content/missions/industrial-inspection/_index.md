## Mission Overview

Industrial facilities present unique challenges: structured but often confined spaces, potential chemical or thermal hazards, and the need for regular, repeatable inspections. ALTER's rovers provide a safer alternative to human inspectors in these environments.

The rover navigates autonomously through pre-mapped facilities, collecting visual, thermal, and atmospheric data. Machine learning models flag potential anomalies — from micro-cracks to gas leaks — and compile structured reports for maintenance teams.

## Validation Protocol

Industrial inspection scenarios are validated in simulated facility environments that include:

- **Confined pipe traversal** — navigating through narrow cylindrical passages
- **Elevated platform inspection** — accessing multi-level structures via ramps
- **Thermal hotspot identification** — detecting temperature anomalies in machinery
- **Gas leak localization** — pinpointing the source of simulated leaks using sensor fusion

Performance metrics include coverage completeness, anomaly detection rate, false positive rate, and total inspection time.
