## Mission Overview

Training and demonstration missions are the backbone of ALTER's iterative development process. Every hardware revision and software update is validated through controlled, repeatable test scenarios before being deployed in competitive or real-world settings.

These missions also serve as operator training ground — ensuring that every team member can confidently control the rover in both teleoperation and supervised-autonomy modes. Public demos at BRAC University events and national competitions help build community support and inspire future engineers.

## Validation Protocol

Training scenarios include standardized obstacle courses and timed challenges:

- **Navigation accuracy course** — waypoint following with measured deviation
- **Endurance run** — continuous operation to validate battery life and thermal management
- **Operator switching** — seamless handoff between operators under time pressure
- **Live demo rehearsal** — full presentation run-through including setup, operation, and teardown

Success criteria include task completion rates, operator response times, system availability percentage, and audience engagement metrics.
