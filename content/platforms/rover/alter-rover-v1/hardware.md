## Chassis & Mobility
- Modular aluminum frame with reinforced mounts
- All-terrain wheel system optimized for rubble and uneven surfaces
- Suspension designed for stability during obstacle traversal

## Sensors
- 2D/3D LiDAR for mapping and obstacle detection
- Depth & RGB cameras for perception
- IMU and wheel encoders for state estimation

## Compute & Power
- Embedded compute unit for autonomy and perception
- Distributed microcontroller system for motor and sensor control
- Power-efficient battery management system for extended missions
